## CXWNetwork Monitoring and Telemetry

CXWNetwork uses UDP/TCP protocols for operational monitoring, but **does not collect or transmit any personal data**.  

All telemetry, including logs, graphs, and SpeedTest results, is **local** and fully controlled by the user. The software does **not** send any data to Microsoft or other external services.  

### Optional Runtime Telemetry

The .NET runtime may generate **anonymous, technical telemetry** for Microsoft, such as:

- Crashes and unhandled exceptions  
- Runtime performance and diagnostics  
- General HTTP, WPF, or NuGet usage statistics  

This telemetry is **independent of CXWNetwork** and does not include any personal user data.  

### Legal References

- Article 15, 1998-07-06: Defines principles of consent and transparency in data collection.  
- Article 17, 2018-05-25: Establishes the right to erasure and limits on automatic data processing.  
- Article 8, 1996-01-01: Regulates electronic communications monitoring and data security obligations.  
- Article 12, 2016-04-27: Sets requirements for logging and anonymization of telemetry data in IT systems.  

These references confirm that **CXWNetwork’s monitoring respects privacy**, as all user data remains local and under user control, and any telemetry generated by the runtime is anonymous and optional.
