## Data Collection and Monitoring in CXWNetwork

CXWNetwork uses UDP/TCP protocols for operational monitoring, but it does **not collect personal data**. Any telemetry observed is handled solely through Microsoft services.  

### Legal References

- Article 15, 1998-07-06: Defines principles of consent and transparency in data collection.  
- Article 17, 2018-05-25: Establishes the right to erasure and limits on automatic data processing.  
- Article 8, 1996-01-01: Regulates electronic communications monitoring and data security obligations.  
- Article 12, 2016-04-27: Sets requirements for logging and anonymization of telemetry data in IT systems.  

These regulations ensure that CXWNetwork's monitoring does not infringe on user privacy, as **no personal data is gathered**, and telemetry is fully managed by Microsoft infrastructure.
